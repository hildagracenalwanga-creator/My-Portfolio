Professional Summary

I am a dedicated human services professional with a Post Graduate Certificate in Human Services focused on Children and Families from Southeastern University and a Bachelor of Arts in Social Development from Makerere University. My professional journey reflects a strong commitment to strengthening families, protecting children, and supporting communities through compassionate service, leadership development, and practical training.

My current work centers on Safe Steps Training Services, where I contribute to leadership development, child safeguarding awareness, and mentorship initiatives designed to equip families, caregivers, schools, and community leaders with practical tools to protect and nurture children. Through this platform I design training resources, support safeguarding capacity building, and facilitate leadership discussions that encourage ethical leadership and responsible community engagement. This work also includes Beyond Success leadership training, which focuses on personal character, values based leadership, and purposeful service within families and communities.

Alongside this work, I am currently undergoing a three-months website development training through Women in Technology Uganda (WITU). This training is strengthening my digital communication skills and is introducing me to practical web development tools that support program visibility, learning resources, and professional portfolio development. This training is also reflecting my commitment to continuous learning and adapting to modern tools that strengthen community focused initiatives.

Before establishing this focus, I served as Project Officer for the Children's Heart Project with Samaritan's Purse, where I supported an international pediatric cardiac referral program. In this role I coordinated complex logistics for children requiring life saving heart surgery, including medical travel arrangements, patient preparation, and family support. I conducted hundreds of home visits before and after surgery to monitor recovery, provide emotional and spiritual support, and ensure children and families received the care and encouragement needed during difficult medical journeys.

My work required collaboration with hospitals, international medical teams, government offices, donors, and community partners. I also maintained detailed program records, safeguarding compliant case files, and monitoring reports that supported program accountability and effective decision making.

Earlier in my career I served as a Document Management Officer with Comtel Integrators Africa on the Stanbic Bank project, where I ensured accuracy, confidentiality, and proper documentation within financial data systems. This experience strengthened my attention to detail, records management skills, and commitment to professional standards.

My professional journey has also been shaped by extensive volunteer service. For more than a decade I have participated in children’s camps organized through Samaritan's Purse and Children After God's Heart, helping create safe and supportive environments for children and teenagers. In these settings I have mentored young leaders, facilitated group discussions, and ensured safeguarding practices are consistently upheld.

Throughout my work I remain deeply committed to child protection, ethical leadership, and community transformation. I believe strong families and informed communities create safe spaces where children can grow, learn, and flourish. My goal is to continue contributing to initiatives that strengthen safeguarding systems, build leadership capacity, and support the well being of children and families across communities.

Professional Achievements

Safe Steps Training Services

Nurturing Leaders, Strengthening Families, Transforming Communities

Safe Steps Training Services is a consultancy and family support organization committed to developing responsible leaders, strengthening families, and supporting communities to create safe, nurturing, and thriving environments. We equip leaders, caregivers, institutions, and communities with mentorship, practical tools, and guidance that foster resilience, integrity, and compassion.

Our Approach

We combine leadership development, family strengthening, and child-centered safeguarding to create lasting transformation. Our programs are evidence-based, culturally relevant, and holistic, addressing emotional, social, and practical needs.

  • Mentorship & Leadership Formation: Empowering youth, parents, and community leaders to guide others with integrity and wisdom.
  • Family Strengthening: Equipping families with skills, emotional support, and practical resources to create stable, nurturing homes.
  • Child Safeguarding & Development: Ensuring safe environments through training, policy support, and structured child-focused programs.
  • Interactive Learning Tools: Books, games, puzzles, and resource kits that encourage reflection, critical thinking, and collaborative learning.

Our Programs

  1. The NurtureOrbit Collective – Leadership & Mentorship Development: Mentorship and leadership programs for youth, young adults, parents, and community leaders, supported by learning resources that strengthen character, decision-making, and life skills.
  2. Family Strengthening & Parenting Support: Parenting education, family support, financial empowerment, mental wellness, and substance use recovery programs that help families thrive.
  3. Child Safeguarding & Development: Safeguarding training, policy development, institutional assessments, and child-focused programs that ensure safe, supportive, and nurturing environments.

Our Impact

  • Empowered Leaders: Youth, parents, educators, and community leaders gain skills and confidence to lead with integrity.
  • Stronger Families: Families acquire tools and support to provide nurturing, stable, and resilient homes.
  • Safe Environments for Children: Schools, churches, and communities implement safeguarding practices that protect and support children.
  • Thriving Communities: Collaboration, shared responsibility, and collective care become the foundation for lasting social impact.

At Safe Steps, we believe lasting transformation begins with empowered people, strong families, and communities united in care and responsibility.
